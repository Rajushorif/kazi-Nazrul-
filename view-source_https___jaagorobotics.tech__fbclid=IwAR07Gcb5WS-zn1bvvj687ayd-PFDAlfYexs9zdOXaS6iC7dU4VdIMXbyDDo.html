<h2 class="mb-4">Providing Robotics Education</h2>
<p>
We teach Robotics, Coding, and STEM to underpriveledged students
in Bangladesh at the Jaago Foundation. As a branch of the Jaago
Foundation, we use the Lego Mindstorms EV3 Robotics kit to help

provide new opportunities for students who don't have access to
them.

<span class="text-primary">About The Program</span>
<h2 class="mb-4">
Our goal is to expand Robotics and Coding in Bangladesh.
</h2>
<p>
We go once every year to teach Robotics and Coding to the bright
students at the Jaago Schoool in Dhaka. We have gone twice so
far, in the summer of 2018 and winter of 2019.
</p>
</div>

<h3 class="mb-5">Promoting Robotics and Skills</h3>
<p>
We aim to teach the fundamentals of Robotics and Coding to
students who have no exposure. As a branch of Jaago, we want
to teach motivated and inspired students who want to have new
opportunites in often unaccessible fields, such as Computer
Science and Engineering.
</p>
<p>
The exposure given to the students by us will open them up to
new fields which will open opportunities which they would not
have had before.
</p>

<div class="container site-section" id="accomplishments">
<div class="row justify-content-center text-center mb-5">
<div class="col-md-6">
<span class="text-primary">Projects</span>
<h2 class="mb-4">The Projects and Achievements of the Students</h2>
<p>
The Jaago students learned very quickly and achieved their goals
and tasks in a very fast manner.
</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row justify-content-center">
<div class="col-lg-7">
<div class="row">
<div class="col-lg-6 mr-auto pr-lg-5">
<div class="feature-3">
<h3>Levi's Presentation</h3>
<p>
In between our first and second group of students, the first
group gave a presentation to Levi's, the denim company, in
the hopes of receiving funding for their school. The
students showed such a strong understanding of the material
that they were given the grant and funds for their school.
</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="col-lg-6 ml-auto pl-lg-5">
<div class="feature-3">
<h3>Line Follower</h3>
<p>
For the final project, the students learned to code and
implement a line follower robot, which followed a black
line. Students used their new-found coding and engineering
skills to create their robot. After lots of testing, all
groups were able to achieve the task.
</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="col-lg-6 mr-auto pr-lg-5">
<div class="feature-3">
<h3>Board Presentation</h3>
<p>
For the last session with our groups the students gave a
presentation to the JAAGO Board explaining the work they
have done in the past two weeks, and why it matters. This
was a huge success as the students were able to do this
without the help of us, or their teacher.
</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="col-lg-6 ml-auto pl-lg-5">
<div class="feature-3">
<h3>Continued Achievement</h3>
<p>
Even though our in-person sessions are limited, the students
wanted to continue to learn new concepts. Therefore, with
the help of faculity, we were able to establish weekly
meetings where we would send lesson plans and long term
projects. The next project they began was a color sorting
robot.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
